ABOUT ME

Hey! I'm Vang, hailing from An Giang, Vietnam. My goal is to craft games that help players unwind and refresh their minds, much like a gentle, cool stream.

Let's be real: commercial game development is tough, especially for solo indie devs. My journey has been even steeper - learning everything from zero. No formal education in the field, no mentors to guide me, working with a tight budget and basic gear... But you know what? Those limitations don't hold me back. They actually add flavor to my creative process!

Seven months after first diving into Unity and game dev, I launched my debut title "Endless Bounce: Sweet Breaker" on Google Play. This is just the beginning - more games are on the way. I have stories to tell and messages to share. Games are my perfect medium for this.

Your support would mean the world to me!
Cheers!
